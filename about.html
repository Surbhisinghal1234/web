Our History

Shree Ratan Industries, a pioneering firm founded in 1985 by Mr. Mahendra Jain. With humble beginnings rooted in the production of double cotton coated copper wire (DCC copper wire), our journey has been one of continuous evolution and growth. Over the years, we have diversified our offerings to encompass the entire spectrum of bare copper wire solutions, catering to the dynamic needs of the electrical industry. At Shree Ratan Industries, we take pride in our unwavering commitment to quality and innovation. Our products are meticulously crafted using high-grade raw materials and cutting-edge production techniques, ensuring unparalleled performance and reliability in every application.

Meet Our Managing Director, Mr. Anshul Jain

With a stellar reputation as a leading expert in the copper wire industry, Mr. Anshul Jain brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to MISTEL. A graduate of MNIT Jaipur in engineering and IIM Calcutta in management, Mr. Jain embarked on his journey with Shree Ratan Industries in 2013. His profound expertise and strategic insights into metal and machinery have played a pivotal role in the company's diversification and growth. Mr. Jain is a firm advocate of KAIZEN, a Japanese philosophy that champions continuous improvement in business practices, ensuring that MISTEL remains at the forefront of innovation and excellence in the industry.

CERTIFICATIONS

Mr. Anshul Jain's contributions to the industry have been widely acknowledged, earning him prestigious accolades such as the Youth Icon Award 2021. Presented by esteemed dignitaries including Shri Narayan Rane (Central Minister of MSME Industries and ex-Chief Minister of Maharashtra), Shri Satish Poonia (President of BJP, Rajasthan), and Shri Ramcharan Bohra (Member of Parliament), this award underscores Mr. Jain's exemplary leadership and dedication to excellence. Additionally, Mr. Jain holds the esteemed certificate of Executive Member of FORTI Youth Wing further cementing his status as a respected leader in the industry.
